About

GivinGallery is where artists can display and sell art to support charitable causes. Many artists struggle to balance their creative ambitions with their financial needs, and many would also like to make a positive difference in the community through their work. We believe that artwork inspired by social causes has a higher intrinsic value, and would sell at a higher price, benefiting both the artist and the charity. We will create this mutually beneficial relationship that will increase awareness of the artists and the causes that inspire them.
